# Market Product

Create a complete product marketing toolkit from positioning to channel-specific copy.

## Step 1: Gather Context

If not provided, ask:
- What is the product and what does it do?
- Who is the primary target customer?
- Who are the main competitors?
- What stage is the product? (Pre-launch / post-launch / re-positioning)
- What channels will this messaging be used in?

## Step 2: Define Positioning

Activate the **positioning-ideas** skill.

Map competitive alternatives, unique attributes, and value. Generate positioning options across all four approaches. Recommend the strongest positioning strategy.

## Step 3: Write Value Prop Statements

Activate the **value-prop-statements** skill.

Generate channel-specific statements for:
- Landing page headline
- Elevator pitch
- Sales email opener
- Onboarding welcome message
- Investor pitch (if needed)
- Tweet / short form

## Step 4: Build the Messaging Architecture

Organize all copy into a messaging hierarchy:

```
PRIMARY MESSAGE (landing page headline)
    └── [Tagline]

SUPPORTING MESSAGES
    ├── For [Segment 1]: [Tailored message]
    ├── For [Segment 2]: [Tailored message]
    └── For [Segment 3]: [Tailored message]

PROOF POINTS
    ├── [Customer quote]
    ├── [Metric / stat]
    └── [Use case / case study hook]
```

## Step 5: Message Testing Plan

Recommend 2–3 A/B tests for the most critical message:
- What variable to test (headline vs. subheadline vs. CTA)
- Which channel to test on first
- What conversion metric to optimize

Deliver the complete messaging toolkit as a document ready to share with design and marketing teams.
