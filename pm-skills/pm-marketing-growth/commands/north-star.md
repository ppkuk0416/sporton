# North Star

Define the North Star Metric that will align your product team around a single measure of customer value.

## Step 1: Understand the Business

If not provided, ask:
- What does the product do?
- B2B or B2C?
- How does the business generate revenue?
- What's the core value delivered to customers?
- What metrics are currently being tracked?

## Step 2: Define the NSM

Activate the **north-star-metric** skill.

Classify the business game (Attention / Transaction / Productivity). Generate 3–5 NSM candidates. Score each against the 7 criteria. Recommend the best NSM with a precise operational definition.

## Step 3: Build the Input Metrics Tree

For the recommended NSM, identify 3–5 input metrics that directly drive it.
For each input metric:
- Current value (baseline)
- Target value (12-month goal)
- Which product area or team owns it
- Top 2 initiatives that could move it

## Step 4: Connect to OKRs

Show how the NSM connects to company OKRs:
- Which company OKR does this NSM support?
- How does improving the NSM translate to business outcomes?

## Step 5: Deliver NSM Framework

One-page NSM document:
- **NSM**: [Name and definition]
- **Current value**: [Baseline]
- **12-month target**: [Goal]
- **Input metrics**: [Tree with baselines and targets]
- **NSM owner**: [Team responsible for this metric]
- **Review cadence**: [Weekly / Monthly]
- **Dashboard link**: [Where to find this metric]
