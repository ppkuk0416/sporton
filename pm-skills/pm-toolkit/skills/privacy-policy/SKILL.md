# Privacy Policy

Use when: the user needs a privacy policy for a website, app, or digital product. Generates a clear, compliant-oriented privacy policy covering the major regulatory frameworks.

Trigger: "privacy policy", "data policy", "GDPR compliance", "CCPA", "data collection policy", "terms of service"

## Framework

### What a Privacy Policy Must Cover

A comprehensive privacy policy should address:

#### 1. Data Collected
- What personal data do you collect?
  - Identifiers: Name, email, phone, IP address, device ID
  - Usage data: Pages visited, features used, session duration
  - Transaction data: Purchase history, payment method (not full card numbers)
  - Location data: If collected
  - Cookies and tracking
- How is it collected? (Directly from user / automatically / from third parties)

#### 2. Purpose of Data Collection
For each data type, specify the legal basis and purpose:
- **Contractual necessity**: Required to provide the service
- **Legitimate interest**: Business analytics, fraud prevention
- **Consent**: Marketing emails, non-essential cookies
- **Legal obligation**: Tax records, compliance requirements

#### 3. Data Sharing
- Do you share data with third parties?
- Name the categories of recipients (analytics providers, payment processors, cloud services)
- Is data shared internationally? If yes, what safeguards are in place?

#### 4. Data Retention
- How long do you keep personal data?
- Policy for deleting data after account closure

#### 5. User Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

**GDPR Rights (EU users):**
- Right to access: Request a copy of their data
- Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate data
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object
- Rights related to automated decision-making

**CCPA Rights (California users):**
- Right to know what data is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising rights

#### 6. Cookies
- What cookies do you use? (Essential / Functional / Analytics / Marketing)
- Cookie consent mechanism
- How to opt out

#### 7. Security
- What security measures protect the data?
- How are breaches handled?
- How quickly are users notified of breaches?

#### 8. Children's Privacy
- Do you collect data from children under 13? (COPPA)
- If not, state so explicitly

#### 9. Contact Information
- Data controller name and contact
- DPO (Data Protection Officer) contact if applicable
- How to submit rights requests

#### 10. Policy Updates
- How users are notified of changes
- Effective date of current policy

## Output Format

Generate a complete privacy policy in plain English (not legal jargon). Structure:
1. Introduction (who we are, what this covers)
2. Data we collect (table format: Type / How collected / Purpose)
3. How we use your data
4. Data sharing (with whom and why)
5. Your rights (GDPR and/or CCPA sections as applicable)
6. Cookies policy
7. Security
8. Children's privacy
9. Changes to this policy
10. Contact us

**Important disclaimer to include**: "This policy is generated as a starting template. Consult a qualified legal professional before publishing, especially if operating in regulated industries or jurisdictions with specific requirements."
