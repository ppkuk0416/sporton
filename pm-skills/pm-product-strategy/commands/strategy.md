# Strategy

Build a complete product strategy document from scratch.

## Step 1: Gather Context

If not provided, ask:
- What is the product and what does it do?
- Who is the target customer?
- What stage is it at? (idea / early / growth / mature)
- What's the business goal for the next 12 months?
- Who are the main competitors?

## Step 2: Define Product Vision

Activate the **product-vision** skill.
Generate a vision statement and narrative using the Geoffrey Moore and Marty Cagan frameworks.

## Step 3: Map the Value Proposition

Activate the **value-proposition** skill.
Build the customer profile (jobs, pains, gains) and value map. Assess fit. Output a crisp value proposition statement.

## Step 4: Assess Strategic Position

Activate the **swot-analysis** skill.
Run a SWOT + TOWS analysis. Identify the top 5 strategic priorities.

## Step 5: Design the Business Model

Activate the **business-model** skill.
Map the Business Model Canvas. Stress-test the revenue model and surface top vulnerabilities.

## Step 6: Set Pricing Direction

Activate the **pricing-strategy** skill.
Recommend a pricing model, tiers, and psychological framing. Include a competitive price-value map.

## Step 7: Deliver the Strategy Document

Produce a structured strategy doc:

---
**[Product Name] — Product Strategy**

**Vision**: [One-sentence vision]

**Customer**: [Primary segment]

**Value Proposition**: [2–3 sentence VP statement]

**Strategic Priorities** (next 12 months):
1. [Priority 1]
2. [Priority 2]
3. [Priority 3]

**Business Model**: [Key revenue streams + model type]

**Pricing**: [Model + tier summary]

**Key Risks**: [Top 3 strategic risks]

**Next Milestones**: [3 most important near-term milestones]
---
