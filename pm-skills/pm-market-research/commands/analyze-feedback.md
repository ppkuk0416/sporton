# Analyze Feedback

Extract insights from customer feedback through structured sentiment and theme analysis.

## Step 1: Gather Feedback

Ask for the raw feedback if not provided:
- Where is the feedback from? (Reviews, surveys, support tickets, interviews, NPS responses)
- How many items are there?
- What time period does it cover?
- Is there a specific question you're trying to answer?

If feedback is provided, confirm the source and volume.

## Step 2: Run Sentiment Analysis

Activate the **sentiment-analysis** skill.

Categorize each piece of feedback by sentiment. Extract themes. Score themes by frequency and impact. Identify representative quotes for each top theme.

## Step 3: Cross-Reference with User Personas

If personas are available: Which customer segments dominate the negative feedback? Positive feedback?

Map each top theme to the most affected persona. This clarifies which user type is most impacted and who to prioritize.

## Step 4: Connect to Product Roadmap

For each high-priority negative theme:
- Is this already on the roadmap? (If yes: flag urgency)
- Is this a known issue or surprising? (If surprising: investigate root cause)
- What's the estimated % of customers affected?

## Step 5: Deliver Insights Report

Produce:
1. **Executive Summary** (3 bullets): What the feedback tells us right now
2. **Theme Analysis**: Full breakdown with frequency, sentiment, and quotes
3. **Action List**: Top 5 recommended actions (Fix / Investigate / Track / Celebrate)
4. **Roadmap Impact**: Which open roadmap items are confirmed vs. challenged by this data
5. **Next Research Steps**: What to ask in the next round of interviews to dig deeper
