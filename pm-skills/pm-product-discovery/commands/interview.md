# Interview

Generate a complete, structured customer interview guide.

## Step 1: Clarify Research Goals

If not provided, ask:
- What product or domain are you researching?
- What's the main question you want answered? (e.g., "Why do users churn after week 2?")
- Who is the target interviewee? (role, experience level, segment)
- Is this discovery (open-ended) or validation (testing a hypothesis)?

## Step 2: Generate the Interview Script

Activate the **interview-script** skill.

Customize the script based on:
- Research goal (discovery vs validation)
- Target interviewee persona
- Specific areas to probe (if provided)
- Time available (30 / 45 / 60 min)

## Step 3: Add Hypothesis Probes (if Validation)

If the user has a specific hypothesis to test, add 2–3 targeted story prompts that will surface evidence for or against it — without leading the interviewee.

## Step 4: Provide Facilitation Tips

Include a facilitation cheat sheet:
- How to redirect when interviewees talk about solutions
- How to dig deeper when answers are vague
- How to handle quiet interviewees
- Signs of a high-signal response

## Step 5: Interview Debrief Template

Provide a one-page debrief template to fill out immediately after each interview:

```
Interview Debrief — [Date] — [Interviewee Role]

Biggest surprise:
Strongest pain point mentioned:
Memorable quote:
Behavior I didn't expect:
Opportunity hypothesis:
Follow-up questions for next interview:
Confidence in hypothesis (before/after): __ / __
```

Remind the user: look for patterns after 5+ interviews before drawing conclusions.
